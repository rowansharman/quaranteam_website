QuaranTEAM

A Telepresence Game

What is QuarranTEAM?

QuarranTEAM is a small form-factor mobile telepresence robot game. Remote users can log in to control the robot, visit their friends, and explore the room to collect as many tags as they can before time runs out!

How Does it Work?

QuarranTEAM uses the Zoom remote conferencing platform to send live video between the robot and the remote user's computer. A Python Socket server sends commands from the remote computer to control the robot. An NFC reader collects tags around the room when the robot approaches them. The system relies heavily on a custom PIC microcontroller board that acts as a USB device, controlling the motors and the tag reader.

Why?

When the COVID-19 shitstorm hit in Spring of 2020, students at Olin College and around the world were forced to complete the semester remotely. Rowan and Alex formed a team for the final prject in their "Elecanisms" course. This project was their strategy to connect with their friends and complete the course.
